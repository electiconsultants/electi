## Getting Started

### Creating Your Account

1. Visit the Electi Consultants platform
2. Click **"Get Started"** or **"Register"**
3. Fill in your information:
   - **Full Name**: Your complete name
   - **Company Name**: Your organization's name
   - **Email Address**: Your business email
   - **Password**: Create a secure password (minimum 6 characters)
   - **Confirm Password**: Re-enter your password
4. Click **"Create Account"**
5. You'll be redirected to the login page

### Logging In

1. Click **"Login"** from the homepage
2. Enter your email and password
3. Click **"Sign In"**
4. You'll be taken to your personalized dashboard

## Operations Management Tool

### Purpose
Optimize your business operations, identify bottlenecks, and improve efficiency.

### How to Use

#### Step 1: Input Your Data
- **Department**: Enter the department name (e.g., Sales, Marketing, IT)
- **Team Size**: Number of team members
- **Current Efficiency**: Your current efficiency percentage (0-100)
- **Monthly Budget**: Your department's monthly budget in dollars

#### Step 2: Analyze
Click **"Analyze Operations"** to generate insights

#### Step 3: Review Results

**Efficiency Metrics:**
- **Efficiency Score**: Your current operational efficiency
- **Optimization Potential**: How much you can improve
- **Cost Savings**: Estimated monthly savings
- **ROI Timeline**: Expected return on investment period

**Visualizations:**
- **Efficiency Trends**: Historical and projected efficiency
- **Resource Allocation**: How your budget is distributed

**AI Recommendations:**
Review personalized suggestions for:
- Automating repetitive tasks
- Optimizing resource allocation
- Implementing cross-training programs
- Streamlining communication

**Bottleneck Analysis:**
Identify process areas that need attention with priority rankings

### Best Practices
- Update your data monthly for accurate trends
- Implement high-priority recommendations first
- Track improvements over time
- Share insights with your team

## Growth Analytics Tool

### Purpose
Forecast revenue growth, identify market opportunities, and track key performance indicators.

### How to Use

#### Step 1: Input Business Metrics
- **Current Monthly Revenue**: Your current monthly revenue in dollars
- **Total Customers**: Number of active customers
- **Current Growth Rate**: Monthly growth percentage
- **Industry**: Your business industry
- **Target Market Size**: Total addressable market in dollars
- **Number of Competitors**: Direct competitors in your market

#### Step 2: Generate Forecast
Click **"Generate Growth Forecast"** to analyze

#### Step 3: Review Insights

**Growth Projections:**
- **6-Month Projection**: Expected revenue in 6 months
- **12-Month Projection**: Expected revenue in 12 months
- **Market Share Potential**: Achievable market share
- **Growth Confidence**: AI confidence score

**Visualizations:**
- **Revenue Growth Forecast**: Conservative, expected, and optimistic scenarios
- **Customer Acquisition Trends**: Total and new customer projections

**Growth Opportunities:**
Review AI-identified opportunities:
- Market expansion strategies
- Product diversification ideas
- Strategic partnership recommendations
- Digital transformation initiatives

**Market Analysis:**
Evaluate different market segments with opportunity scores

**Key Performance Indicators:**
- **Customer Lifetime Value (CLV)**: Projected customer value
- **Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)**: Estimated acquisition cost
- **Churn Risk**: Predicted customer churn rate
- **Expansion Revenue**: Upsell potential

### Best Practices
- Update metrics quarterly for accurate forecasting
- Focus on high-opportunity market segments
- Balance growth initiatives with available resources
- Monitor KPIs regularly

## Tips & Best Practices

### General Platform Usage

1. **Regular Updates**: Update your data regularly for accurate insights
2. **Action-Oriented**: Implement recommendations systematically
3. **Track Progress**: Use the Recent Activity section to monitor your journey
4. **Holistic Approach**: Use all three tools together for comprehensive insights
5. **Data Quality**: Ensure accurate input data for reliable results

### Security

1. **Strong Passwords**: Use complex passwords with letters, numbers, and symbols
2. **Logout**: Always logout when using shared computers
3. **Privacy**: Don't share your login credentials
4. **Updates**: Keep your browser updated for best security

### Getting the Most Value

1. **Baseline Metrics**: Establish baseline measurements before implementing changes
2. **Prioritize**: Focus on high-impact, low-effort recommendations first
3. **Document**: Keep records of implemented changes and results
4. **Iterate**: Continuously refine your approach based on results
5. **Share**: Collaborate with team members on insights and strategies

## Troubleshooting

### Login Issues

**Problem**: Can't login
- **Solution**: Verify your email and password are correct
- **Solution**: Clear browser cache and cookies
- **Solution**: Try a different browser

**Problem**: Forgot password
- **Solution**: Click "Forgot password?" on the login page
- **Solution**: Contact your administrator for password reset

### Data Not Saving

**Problem**: Analysis results not appearing
- **Solution**: Ensure all required fields are filled
- **Solution**: Check that JavaScript is enabled in your browser
- **Solution**: Try refreshing the page

### Charts Not Displaying

**Problem**: Visualizations not showing
- **Solution**: Ensure you have a stable internet connection (Chart.js loads from CDN)
- **Solution**: Disable browser extensions that might block scripts
- **Solution**: Try a different browser

### Performance Issues

**Problem**: Platform running slowly
- **Solution**: Close unnecessary browser tabs
- **Solution**: Clear browser cache
- **Solution**: Ensure you have a stable internet connection
- **Solution**: Try using Chrome for best performance
